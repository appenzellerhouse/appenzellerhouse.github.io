RC Websites

Introduction

As an RC student, you will need to familiarise yourself with a few key websites that will be used throughout your time as an RC student, and as a student here at Yonsei University. This page will give a brief explanation on each of the websites that you may need, with brief instructions on how to use the respective websites. If you are facing any troubles with the website(s), contact your RA for further assistance.

Career Yonsei

Career Yonsei is the main website which you will be using as an RC student. Signing up for RC events, tracking events you had signed up to, and writing your midterm and final report(s) for the semester. Your RAs will be able to view all the activities you have signed up for, and you will be using this website to submit activity reports after you have completed participating in an activity. You can also use this website to sign up for other RC activities offered by RC curriculum (i.e. special lectures and/or courses) that you may be interested in.

Unfortunately, this website is only offered in Korean, and as such we have prepared this handbook on Career Yonsei in case you are unable to understand what to do.
