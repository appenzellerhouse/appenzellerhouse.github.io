Brief Introduction

Formerly known as Aristotle House, Appenzeller was founded on the basis of Aristotle's theory of the "golden mean" with the motto "The Good Life".
We strive to strike a balance the "Study" and "Play" aspects of our lives to puruse intellectual growth and the joy of life simultaneously without compromise, staying true to Aristotle's "golden mean". To do this, we offer a variety of academic, entertainment, sporting, and community programs to promote intellectual growth, personality growth, and an improvement in physical health to become the best versions of ourselves!
Being one of the only English-speaking houses at Yonsei, you can meet your fellow Zellers from all over the world to enjoy cultural diversity while breaking cultural barriers to forge friendships that last a lifetime.
