The RC Program

Brief Introduction

“RC” stands for “Residential College”, and is an education model that strives to maximize learning effectiveness by providing a creative community education that integrates learning and life. At Yonsei, all freshmen spend their first 2 semesters in the Songdo International Campus taking part in the RC program, taking part in activities hosted by the RC or their designated house throughout their first year. Due to complications with COVID-19, RC activities have been and will be held via online platforms in accordance with social distancing measures.

RC System at Yonsei

Yonsei University's RC operates under a triangular coaching system, comprising an academic advisor, major professor, and residential master (RM) professor. The three professors collaboratively instruct each individual student for their career development. More detailed information can be found on the Yonsei RC website found here.
